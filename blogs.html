Data centers will strain the grid (even with demand response)

Kirill Sirik, Alex Crosier, Ronnie Sircar — Large technology companies have a massive hunger for electricity to power AI and cloud data centers. Using grid simulation software Vatic, we studied the impact of 22 GW of projected data center load on the Texas grid. Average wholesale prices rose 49%; demand response helped during peak hours but had limited impact on yearly system costs.
